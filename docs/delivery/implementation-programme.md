EXECUTIVE SUMMARY 

PSL One is envisioned as the Digital Operating System of South African Football. 

Unlike traditional fan engagement applications, PSL One will function as the foundational digital infrastructure layer connecting supporters, clubs, sponsors, broadcasters, ticketing providers, merchants, financial institutions and the Premier Soccer League (PSL) through a single integrated ecosystem. 

PSL One will create a centralised supporter identity framework, enabling measurable engagement, audience intelligence, commerce, sponsorship activation, loyalty participation and long-term ecosystem monetisation. 

The platform will become the official digital gateway through which supporters interact with South African football. 

PSL One will consolidate: 

Fan Registration  

Audience Intelligence  

Loyalty & Rewards  

Ticketing  

Commerce  

Sponsor Activation  

Fantasy Sports  

Content Distribution  

Community Engagement  

Wallet Services  

Marketplace Services  

into a unified digital ecosystem. 

The long-term vision is to establish PSL One as Africa's leading football operating system.

CONCLUSION 

PSL One represents the opportunity to create the digital identity layer, engagement layer, commerce layer and intelligence layer for South African football. 

The platform will unify supporters, clubs, sponsors and commercial partners into a single ecosystem capable of generating sustainable revenue, improving supporter experiences and strengthening the commercial future of football. 

PSL One is designed to become the foundational digital infrastructure platform for South African football and the future launchpad for expansion across the African football ecosystem.
