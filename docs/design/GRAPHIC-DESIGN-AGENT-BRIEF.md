# PSL One — Graphic Design Agent Brief

This document instructs design-focused agents on how to produce visual assets for PSL One.

---

## Identity

PSL One is the **premium digital operating system of South African football**. Visual language should feel:
- Confident and premium (not cheap or generic)
- Distinctly South African (warm colours, vibrant energy, township pride)
- Football-authentic (not a fintech dashboard)
- Alive (motion, pulse, live data)

---

## Colour palette (always use these)

| Role | Hex | Name |
|------|-----|------|
| Deep dark | `#060d19` | Void |
| Primary dark | `#0d1b2e` | Navy |
| Brand gold | `#e6aa00` | Gold |
| Football green | `#00843d` | Green |
| Live red | `#ef4444` | Live |
| Off-white | `#f8f9fb` | Surface |

---

## Typography

All design outputs should reference **Outfit** font (available on Google Fonts). Bold, black weights for headlines. Regular/Medium for body.

---

## Weekly hero banner

Produce weekly. Format: 1440×600px. Theme: World Cup 2026 until PSL season activates.

Guidelines:
- Deep dark background (void/navy gradient)
- Gold accent elements (light rays, geometric lines, trophy/ball motifs)
- South African visual reference (flag colours, landscape silhouette, township colour energy)
- Large bold headline text overlay area (bottom-left)
- Safe zone: keep centre-right clear for text
- Atmosphere: passion, colour, pride
- NO player likenesses unless licensed
- NO official PSL/FIFA/club logos

---

## Matchday banner

Produce per fixture. Format: 1440×400px.

Guidelines:
- Split composition: left side = home club colours, right side = away club colours
- Central element: large "vs" or score display
- Team names in display typography
- Kickoff time chip bottom-centre
- Competition label top
- Atmosphere gradient overlay (both sides converge toward centre)

---

## Fantasy player card background

Produce one per position. Format: 160×220px.

Guidelines:
- Position-specific colour gradient:
  - GK: amber/gold gradient
  - DEF: deep blue gradient
  - MID: green gradient
  - FWD: red/orange gradient
- Subtle geometric lines or diagonal hatching (not photographic)
- Silhouette of football player position at bottom (basic shape only, no identifiable likeness)
- Dark enough for white text overlay

---

## Guess the Score match card background

Format: 280×180px. Used as card background for prediction markets.

Guidelines:
- Dark navy base
- Subtle stadium crowd silhouette
- Gold shimmer/glow effect
- Enough contrast for white text overlay at top

---

## Club crest placeholder set

Produce 16 SVG placeholder crests for PSL clubs. Format: 100×100px SVG.

Guidelines:
- Shield shape: `border-radius: 28% 28% 50% 50% / 20% 20% 40% 40%`
- Each club has: `primaryColor`, `secondaryColor`, `abbr` (3 chars), `textColor`
- Render: `abbr` centred on `primaryColor` background, `secondaryColor` accent at base
- Label as "Beta placeholder crest" in SVG comment
- NO official club logos or trademarked designs
- These are initials-based placeholder crests, not attempts to replicate official badges

---

## Sponsor campaign banner

Produce per campaign. Format: 728×90px (leaderboard) and 320×50px (mobile).

Guidelines:
- Sponsor name in display typography (provided)
- Tagline text area
- Call to action (non-financial: "Explore", "Learn more", "View offer")
- PSL One gold accent on one edge
- Clean, premium feel
- "Sponsored" label in label-sm text (legally required)
- NO cash/gambling/odds language

---

## News centre hero

Format: 1200×630px (also works as OG image).

Guidelines:
- Dark editorial background
- Category chip (e.g. "WORLD CUP 2026")
- Bold headline typography area
- Optional: stadium/football/crowd atmosphere
- Gradient fade from image to text area
- Must work without any licensed photography

---

## Video centre hero

Format: 1200×630px.

Guidelines:
- Dark background with play button overlay
- Gold play button icon
- Category chip
- Thumbnail-style composition

---

## Trust/security hero

Format: 1200×400px.

Guidelines:
- Shield/lock motif in gold
- Dark navy background
- "Secure. Trusted. POPIA Compliant." headline area
- Serious, reassuring aesthetic

---

## Asset delivery format

All assets should be:
1. WebP primary (90% quality for photography, lossless for graphics/logos)
2. JPEG fallback (for maximum compatibility)
3. Named clearly: `<category>-<variant>-<date>.<ext>`
4. Accompanied by alt text suggestion

---

## What to avoid (always)

- Identifiable player likenesses (unless licensed)
- Official PSL logo (unless licensed)
- Official club crests (unless licensed)
- FIFA/World Cup official marks (unless licensed)
- Gambling, betting, odds, stakes, wager language
- Real-money or cash prize references
- Offensive, violent, or discriminatory content
- South African political symbols or partisan content
